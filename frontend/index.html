Welcome to Combat Chronicles!

Dive into a glorious mess of creative chaos where sentient pasta can beat ancient dragons, slippery goblins can steal crowns, and clumsy diplomats might still persuade their way to victory.

This isn't meant to be the most balanced game (yet). It's about having fun with bizarre combinations, weird traits, and unlikely victories. Build your character, enter the fray, and see how far your chaotic genius can take you!

Use the links above to explore your options, start a match, or manage your profile.
